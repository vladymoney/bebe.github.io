Instructor

Chris Pilov

Hello,
My name is Chris Pilov and I am certified baby swimming, massage and yoga instructor for babies from Birthlight.
As a child, I loved hiking in the mountains, swimming and yes I do sports. In my native Sandanski, we have water, a mountain, and air. This ignited my passion for movement, and when I grew up, I also realized the benefits of it.

Convinced that sport is a way of life, I majored in Physical Education education and sports. I started working with children in a private British high school and I didn't feel how while I was cultivating the love for sports u them, discovered my true love - working with children!

Sometime around that time I realized that it is not necessary for the child to grow up to play sports. That's how I started teaching "swimming for babies" courses.

The thought that child can improve physically and emotionally condition since infancy and that I could help him with that, gave me wings!

That's how I discovered Birthlight. Founded in 2000 in the United Kingdom kingdom, they have globally recognized programs and certifications for instructors.

My certification as a baby swim instructor, massage and yoga for babies from Birthlight was a turning point in mine career.

Their mission to create a suitable environment for children in the water became my mission! Their support to the instructors as me, she was truly inspiring!

Now, as part of the Birthlight community, I have the opportunity to I share knowledge and experience with other instructors and parents from all over world, which enriches and strengthens our common mission - yes we support children's development through water and movement!

Today, as the founder and leading instructor of our school for baby swimming, I feel blessed to be able to shares her passion and knowledge with other parents and their little ones aquatic explorers.
